Steve Jobs



Pioneer of The Personal Computer Era

Notable Things Steve Jobs Is Famous For:

Apple
Technology company that specializes in consumer electronics founded in April of 1976.


The iPhone
A smartphone that revolutionalized cellular devices since the first iteration came out in 2007.


The Macintosh
First popular all-in-one desktop personal computer with graphical user interface and a mouse.


The iPod
A handheld computer hard drive which enabled users to store playback music which first launched in 2001.
